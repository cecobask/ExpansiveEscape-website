Mary

Gave The CLiff House Hotel a rating of:

A hotel that really is as lovely as the pictures. Breakfast in the room was one of the loveliest experiences and dinner the night before was a standout dining experience. Living in London we have been to some lovely restaurants but this was superb.


This upscale modern hotel overlooking Ardmore Bay is 17 km from Youghal Golf Club and 23 km from the market town of Dungarvan.

The posh, sea-inspired rooms and suites have water views and artwork by local artisans. They include free Wi-Fi, minifridges, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemaking equipment; some rooms add balconies or terraces. Suites have living spaces, and upgraded suites add split-level floorplans, fireplaces and antique furniture. There's also a cottage with 3 bedrooms.

Breakfast is included at the chic restaurant that offers contemporary Irish fare. There's also a spa with an indoor pool.

FACILITIES

From the privacy of your own balcony complete with table and chairs, you can admire the open vistas around you. You might even spot a Minke Whale or a nesting Kittiwake while you take in pretty Ardmore Bay.


The House Restaurant at Cliff House Hotel carries a Michelin star, with simple principles espoused by chef Martijn Kajuiter. He sources as much produce as possible from Ireland, be it land or sea.


A 15m indoor infinity-edge swimming pool is The Well’s centrepiece. The floor-to-ceiling windows and ocean views create a sense of unending space, as if the pool is merging into the Irish Sea.
