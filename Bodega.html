Bodega Restaurant, Waterford, serving a mix of Classic Irish & European Dishes using the best of local Irish produce in a fun & relaxed atmosphere

In the heart of Waterford City is a restaurant that champions the local food experience. From the beginning, Bodega have strived to offer the best of local produce where possible, to give value for money, to have fun, to provide great food and drinks & to have relaxed customers who can really let themselves go!!

Waterford is a hotbed for fresh, world class quality local produce from farmer to fisherman. The finest seafood & shellfish from Dunmore East, Kilmore Quay & Castletownbere, to the most succulent of local beef or organic pork from The Comeragh Mountains, "Free range" chicken from Wexford, and the famous "Blaa" from an artisan baker 500 metres away! With a Local Tramore man in the Kitchen, Malcolm Starmer , cooking up a storm, and restaurant manager Aneta, in charge of front of house, Bodega can provide you with an experience to remember!
