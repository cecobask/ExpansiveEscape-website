Relaxing

Great food

Comforable in the fresh air

Previous Next

The Beachfront Restaurant is one of the locals hidden gems in the lovely costal town of Tramore. After a long day surfing or just walking along the beach this places is sure to satisfy your hunger. The menu has a wide range of foods from steak , burgers and what they specialize in sea food. freshly sourced only a half an hour away.

After you have eaten there is a lovely outside area where you can sit, have a drink and just enjoy that atmosphere of the area. Suitable for everyone. Families with childeren during the day and more suited to adults at night for the perfect evening meal. The food is very well known amongst the locals and is rated highly. You're very welcome to The Beach Front Restaurant
