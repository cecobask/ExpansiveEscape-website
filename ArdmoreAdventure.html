Ardmore Adventures is the premier outdoor adventure activity provider in the Waterford area. We provide rock climbing, sea kayaking, surfing, snorkelling, archery, team-building, lifeguard training and first aid courses in the unspoilt natural locations of County Waterford.

We're open all year long, catering for groups and individuals. Popular summer camps run through July and August for children aged 8 to 16 years. The camps are a good way for children to learn and experience adventure activities in a fun, safe environment as well as make new friends.

We also cater for stag and hen parties looking for an exciting, memorable day.
