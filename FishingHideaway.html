Peaceful Escape

Great place for fishing

Previous Next

Open for visitors every day!

The lake is a nice relaxing spot for a short walk or for the fisherman in you. The lakes fish stock is controlled and watched carefully so there is always pleanty of fish to be caught. You can choose to keep the fish or throw them back in. You can get fishing supplies in Knockaderry close to the lake.

Around the lake is a walking path that is a nice stroll for those who just want some fresh air or the olympian in you who just wants an open area to run wild. The people here are always friendly and are always up for a good chat and a cup of tea.

Jamie

My name is Jamie and I'm a student. I'm 24 years old, always looking for creative project to work on. I'm currently doing an Arts course and I'm only first year. Whenever I can I like to go into the nature and paint beautiful pictures there. Nature makes me feel relaxed and I can let my creativity and imagination go wild there.

My favourite painting was a painting she drew when she was at the Fishing Hideaway, as it is such a peaceful spot that goes under the radar of many tourists. I recommend to everyone to visit it some day!

Getting there: From Knockaderry, take the Main road south out of the town. Take the first turn right there should be a sign on the bend .

GPS coordinates: 52.207623, -7.277714

Suitability:

Suitable for everyone. Families with their children and pro fishermen are often spotted.

Time: 3 - 6 hours depending on how long you want to stay
